Introduction to Goal 14: Life Below Water

The world's oceans, seas, and marine resources play a crucial role in supporting life on Earth. Sustainable management of these natural resources is vital for the well-being of humanity and the planet. Sustainable Development Goal 14, "Life Below Water," focuses on the conservation and sustainable use of the oceans, seas, and marine resources.

Goal 14 aims to prevent marine pollution, conserve marine biodiversity, and sustainably manage fisheries and other ocean resources. It encourages cooperation at all levels, from local to global, to address the challenges facing our oceans. By achieving Goal 14, we can protect marine ecosystems, support economic development, and maintain the health of the oceans for future generations.

Goal 14: Life Below Water

  • Prevent and significantly reduce marine pollution.
  • Conserve and sustainably use marine resources.
  • Manage and protect marine and coastal ecosystems.
